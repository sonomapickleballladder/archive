The initial registration deadline for the Winter Season is December 30th 2022. (Completing registration by this date makes you eligible to play the first round).

Note: You may still sign-up and join at any time after the initial deadline or after the Ladder is under way (no discounted fee).

Register:

    Step 1. Fill out and submit the Sign-up Form

    Step 2. Make your Payment

    Note: Your registration will not be considered complete until you have completed both of these steps.

Administrative Fees:

    Total your amount due as follows:

    Administrative Registration Fee: $10
    (covers administrative time: getting you into the system, establishing your initial rating and placement, communications)

    PLUS

    Administrative Seasonal Fee: $30
    (covers administrative time: entering scores, forming groups, communications, troubleshooting)

    TOTAL: $40

    Note: These fees have been calculated based on the anticipated initial and weekly time commitment running the Ladder will require of me (the Administrator).

    *** If you wish to add an optional donation amount in recognition of the time I spent doing all the preparatory development work necessary to set up and offer this Ladder, that would be very welcome! ***

How to pay:

    Payment can be made via PayPal, or in person with Theo via cash or check (payable to 'Theo MacKinnon').

    PayPal:

    Please go to PayPal and send to this email address: SPLdeposits@riseup.net (click email to copy). Choose 'Paying for an item or service' so that you are not paying any additional fees.

    Cash/Check:

    Pay me (Theo) or my dad (Adam) whenever you see either of us. (Or click here for times we will be at the courts) (If this is a popular option, I will arrange some times where players can meet me at a certain location [probably the courts]).

Capacity:

    Participation in the Winter Season of the Ladder will be limited to the first 100 players who complete their registration. If I feel I can accomodate a higher number of players once things get underway, I may raise the limit.
