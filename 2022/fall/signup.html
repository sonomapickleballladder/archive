The initial registration deadline for the Fall Season (September - early November) is August 31st.

Note: You may still sign-up any time after August 31st, and even after the Ladder is under way (no discounted fee). If you join the Ladder before 9:00 PM Wednesday on any given week, you will be eligible to play the next week. Otherwise, you won't be eligible until the week after.

Register:

    Step 1. Fill out and submit the Sign-up Form

    Step 2. Make your Payment

    Note: Your registration will not be considered complete until you have completed both of these steps.

Administrative Fees:

    Total your amount due as follows:

    Administrative Registration Fee: $10 (one-time)
    (covers administrative time: getting you into the system, establishing your unique Calendar, establishing your initial rating and placement, communications)

    PLUS

    Administrative Seasonal Fee: $30 (for Fall Season: Sep 5-Nov 6)
    (covers administrative time: entering scores, determining overlapping times, forming groups, communications, troubleshooting)

    TOTAL: $40

    Note: These fees have been calculated based on the anticipated initial and weekly time commitment required of the Administrator to run the ladder.

    *** If you wish to add an optional donation amount in recognition of all the preparatory development work that has been put in during (and before) the beta phase, that would be very welcome! ***

How to pay:

    Payment can be made via PayPal, or in person with Theo via cash or check (payable to 'Theo MacKinnon').

    PayPal:

    Please go to PayPal and send the correct payment amount to this email address: SPLdeposits@riseup.net (click email to copy). Choose 'Paying for an item or service' so that you are not paying any additional fees.

    Cash/Check:

    Pay me (Theo) whenever you see me (or make arrangements to meet via email).

Capacity:

    Participation in the Fall Season of the Ladder will be limited to the first 100 players who complete their registration. If I feel I can accomodate a higher number of players once things get underway, I may raise the limit.
